ABOUT THE PROJECT

During my third semester of college, we were assigned the task of creating a self-branding logo in our graphic design class. I really enjoy minimalistic design, and interesting shapes. I also tried to incorporate the fact that my skills and knowledge are still under construction and are a little rough around the edges. This is the reason why I chose to design an oddly shaped diamond.

A sort of, diamond in the rough concept.
