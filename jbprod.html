ABOUT THE PROJECT

Towards the end of November 2014, a past film student of Confederation College had approached me and asked if I could make them a company logo. Taking into account that they used a camera quite often in their field of work, we had decided to implement a camera aperture into the final design. With the use of colour and contrast, the client's last name initials (JB) are visible in the centre of the aperture.

The final design consists of two versions: A coloured one, as well as a greyscaled one.
