ABOUT THE PROJECT

During my third semester of college, we were assigned the task of creating a video game character for our graphic design class. My game, DreamSpace, was dark and mysterious. A dream being taken over by nightmares essentially.

Melinda, the character I designed, represents the darkness of the nightmares, but in a way that is strong, defensive, and that represents someone who wants to kick ass and defeat their nightmares in order to wake up from their dream.
